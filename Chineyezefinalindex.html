Grassroot Football Academy where stars are born

Our competitive program is for 9 to 14 year old children. Competitive teams are formed CCH based on interest and the commitment of an adult or group of adults willing to take on the responsibilities associated with the team. Competitive teams play in leagues managed by our state soccer association (CCH) and travel to play against teams from surrounding communities.
